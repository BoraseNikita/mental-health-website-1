Get Help

If you or someone you know is struggling with mental health issues, it's important to reach out for help. Here are some resources:

1. National Suicide Prevention Lifeline

If you are in crisis or having thoughts of suicide, call the National Suicide Prevention Lifeline at 1-800-273-TALK (1-800-273-8255). It's free, confidential, and available 24/7.

2. Crisis Text Line

If you prefer texting, you can reach the Crisis Text Line by texting HOME to 741741. A trained crisis counselor will respond to you.

3. Local Mental Health Services

Contact your local mental health services or community health center for information on available resources and support in your area.

Remember, reaching out for help is a sign of strength, and support is available. You are not alone.
